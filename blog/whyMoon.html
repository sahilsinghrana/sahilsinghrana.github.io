Hey! It's all fiction from here. It's all imagination constrained within my head.

Let me chase the moon, maybe along the way I'll touch the sky!

In the midnight's quiet, soft and still,
I chase the moon over hill and hill.
Its silver glow, so distant, bright,
Calls me through the velvet night.

Oh, how I yearn to touch its face,
To close the gap in our endless chase.
Yet bound by chains of earthly ties,
The flight I dream of is nothing but sighs.

I love the moon, its gentle gleam,
A beacon in my darkest dream.
Its light, a whisper, soft and clear,
Keeps my restless heart so near.

But the more I run, the farther it glows,
A dance of shadows that nobody knows.
Each step I take, it slips away,
In the realm of night, a playful dance's sway.

Though I may never hold it tight,
I'll cherish every beam of light.
The moon's soft glow, a gift so rare,
A tender touch, a loving stare.

I've tried to flee its haunting call,
Yet something binds me after all.
A pull so strong, a force unseen,
That draws me to the moon's serene.

In this eternal night, I find,
A solace in the moon's kind mind.
For though its touch I may not feel,
Its glow, its light, forever real.

Beneath the moon's soft glow, I feel the weight,
Of my mistakes, I accept my fate.
I want to say sorry, but fear the reply,
Yet under the moon, I still try.

I know I messed up, I'm ready to learn,
But the thought of forgiveness, I yearn.
Even though it feels like I've done too much wrong,
Beneath the moon, I hope to belong.

Though my capabilities may falter and sway,
I'll strive to improve, day by day.
To offer my best, to honor the moon,
In the quiet dance of night's sweet tune.

-- Hence the Moon.

As soon as you experience magic,
what you considered as facts turns into lies,
what considered as lies turns into belief
